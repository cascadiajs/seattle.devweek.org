Seattle Dev Week

October 17-24 2014

Seattle Dev Week is a week long celebration of the people and companies in Seattle, WA who are building the software of tomorrow. Sign-up below to stay up-to-date on all of the best community-driven meetups, hackathons, workshops, job fairs and parties going on during the week.

If you are joining us from out of town, you'll see exactly what makes Seattle, WA one of the best places for a developer to live and work!

Seattle Scalability Meetup

Wednesday, October 22nd 7:00pm – 9:00pm

WhitePages Inc
1301 5th Avenue #1600

This meetup focuses on Scalability and technologies to enable handling large amounts of data: Distributed Systems, Hadoop, HBase, distributed NoSQL databases, and more!

We are *heavily* focused on deep, technical talks. No marketing pitches, no light use case discussions, no pitches. We want to see architecture diagrams, code, and hear real stories from the trenches.

SeaGL Conference

Friday, October 24th - Saturday, October 25th

Seattle Central College
1701 Broadway Seattle, WA 98122

SeaGL is a grassroots technical conference dedicated to spreading awareness and knowledge about the GNU/Linux community and free/libre/open-source software/hardware. Our goal for SeaGL is to produce an event which is as enjoyable and informative for those who spend their days maintaining hundreds of servers as it is for a student who has only just started exploring technology options.
